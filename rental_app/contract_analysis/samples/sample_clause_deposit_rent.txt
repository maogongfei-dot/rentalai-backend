Part 7 — section A (mixed payment clauses)

1. The tenancy deposit and any holding deposit must be protected in a custodial scheme under tenancy deposit protection rules before the tenancy starts.
2. Monthly rent is £950 pcm and payable monthly in advance on the first day of each month; rental payment by standing order is required.
3. On vacating, the deposit will be returned within 14 days after check-out, subject to lawful deductions for damage beyond fair wear and tear.
