ASSURED SHORTHOLD TENANCY AGREEMENT (EXCERPT — SAMPLE SAFE)

1. Rent: The monthly rent is £950 per calendar month (pcm), payable in advance on the 1st of each month by standing order.

2. Deposit: A tenancy deposit of £1,095 will be held and protected within the DPS custodial scheme within 30 days. The tenant will receive prescribed information.

3. Notice: After the fixed term, either party may end the tenancy by giving one month notice in writing. The notice period shall be not less than one month.

4. Break clause: The tenant may exercise a break option after 6 months by giving two months notice to the landlord.

5. Early termination: Early termination may be agreed in writing; the tenant may surrender the tenancy subject to reasonable costs.

6. Repairs: The landlord shall keep the structure and exterior in repair. The tenant is responsible for minor maintenance and keeping the property in good condition.

7. Council tax and bills: Council tax is payable by the tenant. Utilities (gas, electricity, water charges) are not included in the rent unless stated in the schedule.

8. Access: The landlord or agent may enter to inspect or repair after giving at least 24 hours reasonable notice, except in an emergency.

9. Pets and subletting: No pets without prior written consent. Subletting and assignment are not permitted without the landlord's consent.

10. Inventory: A schedule of condition will be prepared at check-in and check-out.

11. Rent increase: The rent may be reviewed once per year with not less than one month's notice of any increase.
