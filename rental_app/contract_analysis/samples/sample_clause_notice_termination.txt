Part 7 — section B (ending the tenancy)

1. The tenant and landlord must each give two months notice in writing. The statutory notice period and any agreed period of notice apply to both parties under this agreement.
2. A break clause permits early termination after the first six months, provided prior notice is given in line with this agreement.
3. The tenancy may end by surrender of tenancy by mutual consent recorded in writing without implying waiver of other rights.
