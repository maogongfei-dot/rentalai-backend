TENANCY AGREEMENT — SAMPLE MEDIUM RISK (EXCERPT)

Rent: £850 pcm payable monthly in advance.

Deposit: £850 held as security. The parties will discuss deposit protection (TDS) after signing.

Notice: The tenant should give notice when leaving. Termination procedures apply as agreed.

Repairs: The tenant must report repairs. The landlord will address structural issues where reasonable.

Bills: Utilities and council tax are discussed with the agent. Energy and water charges may apply.

Access: The landlord may access for inspections with notice where possible.

Additional charges: A cleaning fee of £120 applies at the end of the tenancy. A late payment fee of £40 per week may be charged if rent is overdue. A penalty may apply for breach of house rules.

Admin fee: An administrative fee of £100 is payable on signing. Check-in fee £50.

Inventory: Check-in and check-out appointments should be arranged with the agent.
