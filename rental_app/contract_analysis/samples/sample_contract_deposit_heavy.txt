ASSURED SHORTHOLD TENANCY — DEPOSIT-HEAVY SAMPLE

The monthly rent is £1,000 pcm payable in advance on the 1st of each month.

The tenancy deposit is £8,000. The landlord confirms the deposit will be protected in the DPS custodial scheme and prescribed information will be provided.

Council tax and utility bills are payable by the tenant. Gas and electricity are not included in the rent.
