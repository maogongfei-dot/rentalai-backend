TENANCY (OMITS NOTICE AND RENT ESCALATION; NO STRUCTURAL DUTIES CLAUSE)

1. Rent: Monthly rent £1,200 pcm, payable on the 1st.

2. Deposit: Tenancy deposit will be protected in the mydeposits insured scheme.

3. Break clause: A break clause allows early termination after 12 months; surrender may be agreed in writing.

4. Bills: Council tax and utility bills (gas, electricity) are excluded from rent.

5. Access: The landlord may enter after reasonable notice to inspect.

6. Inventory: Schedule of condition at check-in and check-out.
