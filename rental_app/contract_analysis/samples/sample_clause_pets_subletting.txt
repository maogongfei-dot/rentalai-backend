Part 7 — section D (occupants and transfers)

1. No pets are allowed in the dwelling without prior written consent; where permitted, a pet fee may be required under the landlord's written policy for animals.
2. Subletting, assignment, or parting with possession of the whole or any part is prohibited without consent; taking in a lodger or sub-let arrangements require the landlord's written approval.
3. Keeping animals in communal areas is not permitted except as agreed in writing; general noise from animals must not cause nuisance to neighbours.
