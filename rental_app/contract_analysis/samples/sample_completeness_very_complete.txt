ASSURED SHORTHOLD TENANCY — VERY COMPLETE (PART 10 COMPLETENESS QA)

1. Rent: The monthly rent is £950 per calendar month (pcm), payable in advance on the 1st of each month by standing order.

2. Deposit: A tenancy deposit of £1,095 will be held. The deposit will be protected in the DPS custodial scheme within 30 days; prescribed information will be provided.

3. Notice: After the fixed term, either party may end the tenancy by giving one month notice in writing. The notice period shall be not less than one month.

4. Break clause: The tenant may exercise a break option after 6 months by giving two months notice.

5. Early termination: Early termination may be agreed in writing; the tenant may surrender the tenancy subject to reasonable costs.

6. Repairs: The landlord shall keep the structure and exterior in repair under section 11. The tenant is responsible for minor maintenance.

7. Bills: Council tax is payable by the tenant. Utilities including gas and electricity are not included unless stated in the schedule.

8. Access: The landlord may enter to inspect or repair after giving at least 24 hours reasonable notice, except in an emergency.

9. Inventory: A schedule of condition will be prepared at check-in and check-out.

10. Rent increase: The rent may be reviewed annually with not less than one month notice of any rent increase.
