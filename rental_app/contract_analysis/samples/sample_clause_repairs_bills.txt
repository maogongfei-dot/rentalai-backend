Part 7 — section C (upkeep and charges)

1. The landlord shall keep the structure and exterior in repair, including the landlord's repairing obligations for heating and hot water; the tenant must report maintenance issues promptly.
2. Council tax and utility bills are the tenant's responsibility; bills excluded from the monthly payment include electricity, water charges, and broadband unless stated as bills included.
3. A schedule of condition and inventory will be agreed at check-in and check-out to record the state of the property.
