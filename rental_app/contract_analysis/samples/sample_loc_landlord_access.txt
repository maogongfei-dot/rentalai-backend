LOCALIZATION SAMPLE — Landlord access without notice (Part 5)

This sample is designed so rules can extract a clear matched_text for entry / access risks.

1. Rent: Nine hundred and fifty pounds per calendar month.

2. Quiet enjoyment: The tenant has quiet enjoyment subject to the following access terms.

3. Inspection and access: The landlord may enter the premises at any time to carry out inspections, repairs, or viewings, and may access without notice when the landlord considers it necessary.

4. The tenant shall not unreasonably withhold consent where the landlord may enter under clause 3, including entry without prior notice in an emergency.
