TENANCY (NO DEPOSIT SCHEME / NO LANDLORD ENTRY CLAUSE)

1. Rent: £950 per month payable monthly in advance.

2. Deposit: A tenancy deposit of £1,095 is payable on signing.

3. Notice: Either party may end by giving one month notice in writing after the fixed term.

4. Repairs: The landlord shall keep in repair the structure and exterior.

5. Bills: Council tax and gas and electricity are payable by the tenant.

6. Termination: Early termination and break clause terms apply as per statute.

7. Inventory: check-in and check-out with schedule of condition.

8. Rent increase: rent review once per year with notice of any rent increase.
