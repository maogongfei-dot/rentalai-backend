LOCALIZATION SAMPLE — Tenant pays all repairs (Part 5)

This sample is designed so rules can extract a clear matched_text for repair responsibility risks.

1. Rent: Eight hundred and eighty pounds per calendar month.

2. Condition: The tenant accepts the property as seen.

3. Repairs and maintenance: The Tenant shall pay for all repairs to the property, including structural repairs, the roof, foundations, and the central heating system.

4. The tenant responsible for all repairs and maintenance costs regardless of cause, except fire or flood solely affecting the building frame where statute requires otherwise.
